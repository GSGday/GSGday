Kontroversi Upin & Ipin Universe: Gaji Karyawan Tak Dibayar & Harga Game yang Mengundang Protes

Azhar Muhazzab 11 Juli 2025 5 menit baca

Game terbaru dari karakter animasi populer Malaysia, Upin & Ipin Universe, menjadi sorotan bukan karena kualitas permainannya, melainkan karena kontroversi yang melingkupi studio pengembangnya. Streamline Studios, developer game ini, dituding tidak membayar gaji karyawan selama berbulan-bulan sambil meminta mereka bekerja lebih keras.

Karyawan Bekerja Tanpa Gaji Berbulan-bulan

Menurut laporan dari berbagai sumber termasuk akun Twitter @kotakgame, Streamline Studios mempekerjakan karyawan selama berbulan-bulan tanpa memberikan upah yang sesuai. Seorang mantan karyawan yang enggan disebutkan namanya mengungkapkan, "Kami diminta untuk tetap bekerja meski gaji tidak dibayar tepat waktu, dengan janji akan diberikan setelah proyek selesai."

"Streamline Studios dilaporkan melakukan PHK besar untuk memotong pengeluaran mereka, tetapi sayangnya sampai sekarang gaji dan hak karyawan belum dibayar. Selain gaji belum dibayar, beban kerja mereka juga ditambah."

- Sumber: Nmia via @kotakgame

Masalah ini bukan pertama kalinya terjadi. Streamline Studios sebelumnya juga telah melakukan pemutusan hubungan kerja (PHK) besar-besaran, namun hak-hak finansial karyawan yang di-PHK pun belum diselesaikan hingga saat ini.

Harga Game yang Mengundang Kritik

Selain masalah internal studio, Upin & Ipin Universe juga menuai kritik karena harga yang ditetapkan untuk game tersebut. Dengan harga $39.99 (sekitar Rp650.000 atau RM169), banyak gamer yang merasa harga tersebut terlalu tinggi untuk kualitas game yang ditawarkan.

Seorang gamer Malaysia, @tmizvv, dengan sarkastik menulis: "Call me miskin whatsoever but RM169 just to walk around in some kampung pendalaman doing a bunch of bs with 2 bald ass boys is absurd."

Di Indonesia, harga tersebut setara dengan hampir Rp650.000 - jumlah yang sangat signifikan mengingat daya beli masyarakat Asia Tenggara yang berbeda dengan negara-negara Barat.

Mereka juga mengimbau warga Malaysia untuk melaporkan studio tersebut kepada pihak berwenang jika memiliki bukti yang cukup.

Insiden Pembredelan di Forum Komunitas

Kontroversi semakin memanas ketika seorang pengguna Steam dari Malaysia dilaporkan di-banned dari forum komunitas Upin & Ipin Universe hanya karena menyatakan keprihatinannya atas perlakuan terhadap karyawan Streamline Studios.

"Sebelumnya, saya berpikir untuk membeli game ini untuk anak saya karena dia sangat menyukai Upin Ipin. Tapi setelah membaca ini... saya lebih baik melupakannya. Tidak akan mendukung perusahaan yang memperlakukan karyawannya seperti ini."

- Pengguna Steam yang di-ban

Insiden ini menuai kecaman dari komunitas gamer yang menilai moderator forum telah bertindak sewenang-wenang dengan membungkam suara kritis.

Masa Depan Upin & Ipin Universe

Dengan berbagai kontroversi yang membayangi, masa depan game Upin & Ipin Universe yang dijadwalkan rilis pada 17 Juli 2025 ini diragukan banyak pihak. Meskipun karakter Upin dan Ipin memiliki basis penggemar yang besar di Asia Tenggara, praktik buruk yang dilakukan oleh pengembangnya dapat menjadi bumerang bagi kesuksesan game ini.

Pemain game semakin sadar akan pentingnya etika pengembangan game dan hak-hak pekerja di industri kreatif. Boikot yang terjadi mungkin akan memengaruhi penjualan game ini, terutama di pasar Malaysia dan Indonesia yang seharusnya menjadi target utama.

The latest game from Malaysia's popular animated characters, Upin & Ipin Universe, has come under scrutiny not for its gameplay quality, but due to controversy surrounding its development studio. Streamline Studios, the developer of this game, has been accused of not paying employee salaries for months while demanding they work harder.

Employees Working Without Pay for Months

According to reports from various sources including Twitter account @kotakgame, Streamline Studios employed workers for months without providing appropriate wages. A former employee who wished to remain anonymous revealed, "We were asked to keep working even though salaries weren't paid on time, with promises they would be paid after the project was completed."

"Streamline Studios reportedly conducted mass layoffs to cut their expenses, but unfortunately, employee salaries and rights remain unpaid. In addition to unpaid salaries, their workload was also increased."

- Source: Nmia via @kotakgame

This is not the first time this issue has occurred. Streamline Studios had previously conducted massive layoffs, but the financial rights of the laid-off employees have yet to be settled to this day.

Game Price Draws Criticism

In addition to internal studio issues, Upin & Ipin Universe has also drawn criticism for its pricing. At $39.99 (approximately Rp650,000 or RM169), many gamers feel the price is too high for the quality of the game offered.

In Indonesia, that price is equivalent to almost Rp650,000 - a very significant amount considering the purchasing power of Southeast Asian communities compared to Western countries.

Boycott Actions and Support for Employees

They also urge Malaysian citizens to report the studio to authorities if they have sufficient evidence.

Censorship Incident in Community Forum

The controversy heated up when a Steam user from Malaysia was reportedly banned from the Upin & Ipin Universe community forum simply for expressing concern over the treatment of Streamline Studios employees.

"Earlier, I was thinking of buying this game for my child because he really likes Upin Ipin. But after reading this... I'd rather forget it. Won't support a company that treats its employees like this."

- Banned Steam user

This incident drew condemnation from the gaming community, who felt the forum moderators acted arbitrarily by silencing critical voices.

The Future of Upin & Ipin Universe

With various controversies looming, the future of Upin & Ipin Universe, scheduled for release on July 17, 2025, is uncertain. Although Upin and Ipin characters have a large fan base in Southeast Asia, the poor practices by its developer could backfire on the game's success.

Gamers are increasingly aware of the importance of game development ethics and workers' rights in the creative industry. The ongoing boycott may affect the game's sales, especially in the Malaysian and Indonesian markets that should be its primary targets.
