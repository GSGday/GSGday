GTA 6 Bakal Gede Banget? Siap-siap Kosongkan Hard Drive!

Ahmad Fauzi 14 Juli 2025 6 menit baca

Buat para gamers yang punya kebiasaan main banyak game sekaligus, bersiap-siaplah! Ukuran instalasi GTA 6 yang diprediksi bakal besar banget bikin banyak orang mulai ngosongin hard drive mereka dari sekarang.

Dulu, aku tuh tipe gamer yang main satu game single player selesai, baru install game lain. Simple banget kan? Tapi jaman sekarang mah beda cerita. Game-game modern ukurannya gede-gede banget, rata-rata di atas 100GB. Jadi PS5 aja cuma bisa muat 5 game AAA kalau beruntung.

"Dulu aku nggak pernah sedih sama ukuran file game. Tapi sekarang? Game-game modern bikin hard drive nangis!"

Baru kemarin aku harus milih antara Assassin's Creed Shadows atau Star Wars Outlaws yang baru setengah selesai, cuma buat kasih tempat buat Astro Bot yang rilis hari ini. Gimana nanti pas GTA 6 rilis tahun depan?

Seberapa Gede Sih Prediksinya?

Rockstar Games belum ngasih angka resmi, tapi liat aja trailer-nya yang epik banget. Dunianya luas, detilnya gila-gilaan. Pasti ukurannya bakal bikin mata melotot.

~150GB
GTA 6 (Prediksi)
~200GB
GTA 6 + Online (Prediksi)
110GB
Call of Duty: Warzone

Di Reddit, banyak banget gamer yang mulai ribut bahas ini. Salah satu user nanya: "Mungkinkah GTA VI jadi game dengan ukuran file terbesar sepanjang masa?"

"Uangku taruh di 200GB+ di hari pertama, plus tambahan 60-80GB pas Online mode rilis nanti"

- Prediksi salah satu gamer di Reddit

Yang lain ada yang jawab: "Siap aja berapa pun ukurannya, yang penting main GTA 6!". Ada juga yang nebak sekitar 100-150GB.

Reaksi Para Gamer

"Hard drive 1TB ku aja sekarang udah penuh. Kayaknya harus beli SSD eksternal nih buat GTA 6!"

"Kalau GTA 6 ukurannya beneran 200GB, mungkin aku harus hapus pacar dulu dari hard drive hidupku. Eh, maksudnya game-game lain!"

Bukan yang Terbesar, Tapi Tetep Gede!

Sebenernya ada beberapa game yang ukurannya lebih gila lagi dari prediksi GTA 6. Tapi tetep aja, 200GB itu bukan angka main-main buat satu game doang.

Fakta menarik: Ark: Survival Evolved dengan semua DLC-nya bisa nyampe 400GB! Itu baru bener-bener monster!

Tapi GTA 6 beda. Ini game yang ditunggu-tunggu sejagat raya gamer. Jadi wajar kalau ukuran segede gaban pun tetep bakal di-download sama jutaan orang.

Siap-siap dari Sekarang!

Tips Ngosongin Hard Drive buat GTA 6:

  • Uninstall game yang udah selesai - Jangan ditimbun, toh bisa install ulang kapan aja
  • Bersihin file sampah - Cache, temporary files, screenshot yang nggak penting
  • Investasi SSD eksternal - Sekarang harganya udah lebih terjangkau
  • Pilih game yang bener-bener mau dimainin - Jangan serakah install banyak game tapi nggak dimainin
  • Manfaatin cloud storage - Backup file-file penting biar nggak makan tempat

Kalau dipikir-pikir, selama 10 bulan ke depan ini mungkin kita harus fokus ngeberesin game-game yang masih nanggung. Biar pas GTA 6 rilis, kita bisa fokus 100% ke game yang satu ini tanpa gangguan.

"Hard drive penuh itu masalah semua gamer. Tapi buat GTA 6? Aku rela hapus semua game lain kalau perlu!"

- Gamer sejati

Yang jelas, apapun ukurannya nanti, para gamer sejati pasti bakal cari cara buat bisa main GTA 6. Entah itu dengan ngosongin hard drive, beli SSD baru, atau bahkan beli console khusus buat game ini doang!

For gamers who like to play multiple games at once, get ready! The predicted huge file size of GTA 6 has many people starting to clear their hard drives now.

I used to be the type of gamer who would finish one single-player game before installing another. So simple, right? But times have changed. Modern games are huge, averaging over 100GB. Even a PS5 can only hold about 5 AAA games if you're lucky.

"I never used to worry about game file sizes. But now? Modern games make my hard drive cry!"

Just yesterday I had to choose between uninstalling my half-finished Assassin's Creed Shadows or Star Wars Outlaws to make room for Astro Bot releasing today. What will happen when GTA 6 releases next year?

How Big Will It Be?

Rockstar Games hasn't given official numbers yet, but just look at that epic trailer. The world is vast, the details are insane. The file size will definitely make your eyes pop.

GTA 6 (Prediction)
~200GB
GTA 6 + Online (Prediction)

On Reddit, gamers are buzzing about this. One user asked: "Could GTA VI become the game with the largest file size of all time?"

"My money's on 200GB+ on day one, plus an additional 60-80GB when Online mode releases later"

- Prediction from a gamer on Reddit

Others replied: "I'm ready for whatever size, as long as I can play GTA 6!" Some guessed around 100-150GB.

Gamer Reactions

"My 1TB hard drive is already full. Looks like I need to buy an external SSD for GTA 6!"

"If GTA 6 is really 200GB, I might have to delete my girlfriend from my life's hard drive first. Oh wait, I mean other games!"

Not the Biggest, But Still Huge!

Actually, there are some games that are even crazier in size than the predicted GTA 6. But still, 200GB is no joke for a single game.

Fun fact: Ark: Survival Evolved with all its DLCs can reach 400GB! Now that's a real monster!

But GTA 6 is different. This is a game that gamers around the world have been waiting for. So it's understandable that even with such a huge size, millions of people will still download it.

Get Ready Now!

Tips to Clear Your Hard Drive for GTA 6:

  • Uninstall finished games - Don't hoard them, you can reinstall anytime
  • Clean up junk files - Cache, temporary files, unnecessary screenshots
  • Invest in an external SSD - Prices are more affordable now
  • Choose games you really want to play - Don't greedily install many games you won't play
  • Utilize cloud storage - Backup important files to save space

Thinking about it, for the next 10 months we should focus on finishing the games we've started. That way when GTA 6 releases, we can focus 100% on this game without distractions.

"A full hard drive is every gamer's problem. But for GTA 6? I'll gladly delete all other games if needed!"

- True gamer

One thing's for sure, whatever the size, true gamers will find a way to play GTA 6. Whether by clearing hard drive space, buying a new SSD, or even getting a console just for this game!
